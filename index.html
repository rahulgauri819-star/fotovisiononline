Our Story
Foto Vision Studio has been serving the Greater Kailash community for over a decade. What started as a small photo studio has grown into Delhi's most trusted destination for professional photo prints and custom framing. We believe every photograph tells a story, and our mission is to help you display those stories with the quality and care they deserve.
Our Mission
To deliver studio-quality photo prints and frames to every home across India — with the craftsmanship of a professional lab and the warmth of a neighbourhood studio.

Shipping & Delivery

📅 Ready Time
• All orders ready within 3 days of placement

📦 Packaging
All prints are wrapped in acid-free tissue paper and shipped in rigid cardboard mailers. Framed orders are bubble-wrapped and corner-protected to ensure safe delivery.
Cancellation & Returns
❌ Cancellation Policy
Since all products are custom-made for you, cancellations can only be accepted within 1 hour of placing the order. After that, production begins and cancellation is not possible. To cancel, WhatsApp us immediately at 9891002677.
🔄 Returns & Reprint
We do not accept returns on custom-printed products. However, if there is:

• A manufacturing defect
• Damage during transit
• Wrong item delivered

We will reprint and redeliver at no cost. Just WhatsApp us a photo of the issue within 48 hours of receiving your order.
📞 How to Raise a Complaint
1. WhatsApp your order number to 9891002677
2. Send a clear photo of the issue
3. We'll respond within 4 hours
4. Reprint dispatched within 2 working days
Terms & Conditions
1. Orders: All orders are custom-made and non-returnable unless defective.

2. Photos: You confirm you own the rights to all photos uploaded. We are not responsible for copyright issues.

3. Quality: We match your uploaded photo quality. Low-resolution uploads may result in less sharp prints — please upload the highest quality original.

4. Delivery: Estimated delivery dates are indicative. We are not liable for courier delays.

5. Payments: All payments are final. Refunds are only issued in case of verified defects.

6. Privacy: Your photos are used solely for printing your order and deleted from our servers after 30 days.

7. Disputes: All disputes subject to Delhi jurisdiction.
